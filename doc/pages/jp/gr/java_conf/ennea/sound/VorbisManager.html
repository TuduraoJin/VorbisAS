class jp.gr.java_conf.ennea.sound.VorbisManager

Available on all platforms

Controls playback and loading of a group of sounds. VorbisAS references a global instance of VorbisManager, but you are free to instanciate your own and use them in a modular fashion. ... @author Tudurao Jin
